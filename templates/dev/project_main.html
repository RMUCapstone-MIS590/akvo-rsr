Bangalor sanitation

Within a peri-urban resettlement cluster

Status

Active

Focus area

Location

North-East Area
Delhi, India

  • Context
  • Key results
  • Goals
  • Description

This project aims to provide safe sanitation facilities for 500 school children by providing 6 private urinal units and 2 urine diverting dry ecosan toilets. A school curriculum on water, sanitation and hygiene amongthe pupils will be developped and executed. View details

Overall goals

  • 8 functioning sanitation systems
  • 500 persons with access to improved sanitation for 10 years
  • 500 persons who receive training / education per year
